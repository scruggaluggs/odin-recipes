Ants on a Log

Description

It's celery with peanut butter.

Oh, and some raisins.

Ingredients

  • 1 thing of celery
  • 1 jar of peanut butter
  • 1 handful of raisins

Steps

  1. cut up your celery to however big you want your logs
  2. fill the celery gap with peanut butter
  3. put some raisins on it
