Homestyle Potato Chips

Description

It's chips.

Regular ol' chips.

Ingredients

  • 4 potatoes
  • 3 tablespoons of salt
  • 1 quart of oil

Steps

  1. slice em and shove em in a bowl of water
  2. drain water and rinse and soak them in some salty water
  3. drain and rinse again and pat them dry
  4. heat oil in a deep-fryer to 365 degrees F
  5. fry em up til theyre golden and remove with slotted spoon
  6. salt em if you want
